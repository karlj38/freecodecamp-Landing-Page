Who are you?

Karl Jones, a Russell Group BSc Physics graduate proudly earned a 2:1 Hons. (achieving a 1st in ten modules) in 2013 from the University of York.

In 2015, he undertook the bold challenge and moved abroad where he worked primarily teaching mathematics and science to British and international students at private schools.

In 2016, Karl set up STEM Tuition as a standard private tuition service alongside his teaching job, to numerous early successes, but he knew that it could be so much more.

Karl returned from the UK in 2018 and recently reopened STEM Tuition back in his hometown, this time to really make a difference…
